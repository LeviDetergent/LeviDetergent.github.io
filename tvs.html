TVs

Premiere Pro
2023

For this Adobe Premiere project, I gathered two video clips featuring a dancing smoke character and an on/off TV effect from YouTube. After obtaining these materials, I edited an image of stacked old TVs with some green screens, replacing the green screens with the video clips using the Ultra Key effect and creating a smooth transition with the film dissolve effect. To achieve a vintage CRT TV/VHS look, I applied various effects like Mosaic, Gaussian Blur, Lens Distortion, and Grid while adjusting color settings like exposure, contrast, white levels, and black levels. The most time-consuming part of the project involved manually keyframing the omni light intensity to create immersive lighting effects. Finally, I added music, ensuring a smooth start and end, using "Goofy Ahh Type Beat Part 2 (Bababooey Phonk) - Memnicus" as the soundtrack.
