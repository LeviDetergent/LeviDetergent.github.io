About the Artist

Hello there! My name is Dat Ngo, and I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art at San Jos√© State University. My work is heavily influenced by pop culture, especially internet culture, video games, and memes, which have shaped my identity and continue to inspire my creativity. I enjoy blending humor, nostalgia, and digital aesthetics to create work that feels fun, personal, and culturally in tune with the world around me.

I primarily use Adobe Photoshop for illustration, and I also have experience with Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Blender, and Substance Painter. I first got into digital art during my junior year of high school after discovering a Digital Media class. Even though I started later than some, I’ve been driven by a strong passion for learning and growing as an artist. Moving forward, I hope to keep expanding my skills across different mediums and create projects that not only entertain but resonate with people who share similar passions.
