About the Artist

Meet Dat Ngo, a student at San Jose State University, where he's working towards a Bachelor of Fine Arts in the Digital Media Arts program. Dat's main tools for his art are Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, and Medibang as well as some knowledge in After Effects.

Dat's journey into art started during his high school junior year when he discovered Digital Media and instantly fell in love with it. Even though he got into it a bit later than some, his passionate teacher and encouraging classmates pushed him to follow his interests. Dat's big dream is to expand his artistic skills by trying out different art forms and using his artwork to send meaningful messages.
