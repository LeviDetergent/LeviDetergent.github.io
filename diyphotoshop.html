Do it Yourself Photoshop with p5.js
2023

This is a do it yourself version of Photoshop that I made using P5.js, a JavaScript for creative coding. Often used by artists to create artworks or even video games.

1 = brush with random size
2 = white brush
3 = brush with randomized settings (color, size)
4 = randomized rectangle brush
5 = a gradient brush
6 = filled the background in dark gray
7 = filled the background in white
8 = add noise
9 = spray brush
0 = change the background to window xp background
g = randomize images
