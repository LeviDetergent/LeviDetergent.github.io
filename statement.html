Artist's Statement

As a digital artist, I like to make appealing work that also represents contemporary society. I have a background in computer science, which gives me the ability to hand-coded this website as well as pushing the boundaries of traditional art and exploring the connection between creativity and technology.

I am pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University, where I'm always looking for fresh ways to infuse my work with joy and humor. I draw inspiration from online culture and its impact on our culture in everything I do, from making meme mashups to creating net art.

My ultimate goal is to become a game designer so that I can use both my creative and technical knowledge to create enjoyable and culturally significant gaming experiences. I want to create games that are fun and also tell stories about my culture. I'm a Vietnamese-American digital artist, and I want to show off the cool stuff about Vietnam and the place I now call home, San Jose. I've seen that there aren't many things that represent my culture while growing up in America, and I want to help change that for the generations that come after me.
