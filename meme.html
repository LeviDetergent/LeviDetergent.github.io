ALways Has Been JoJo
2023

Digital Media         2D Design and Color Concept         Illustration       About Me       Statement

A mashup of three popular memes Zoolander, JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, and Always Has Been. Zoolander and JoJo both share similar traits but yet they are from two different cultures which make this mashup fit very well together and entertaining. Memes have become a major part of the internet culture, and a mashup always a fun way to keep the humor fresh and engaging.
