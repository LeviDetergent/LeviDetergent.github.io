Golden Gate
2021

Digital Media         2D Design and Color Concept         Illustration       About Me       Statement

This composition highlights the environment surrounding the subject matter as a means of expressing different character traits and narrating a story. The calming light orange-blue gradient, paired with a peaceful background, represent the character's calm and lazy demeanor. The lone seagull positioned to the right serves as a symbol of the character's feelings of loneliness and isolation from the group. Furthermore, the rough texture of the short concrete wall symbolizes the character's difficult past.
