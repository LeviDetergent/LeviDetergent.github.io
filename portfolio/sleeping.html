Guitar
2021

Digital Media         2D Design and Color Concept         Illustration       About Me       Statement

In this composition, my focus was on portraying the personality of a human figure through the use of color and posture. The mauve color scheme I used represents the girl's friendly, kind, and generous nature, while her posture and surroundings convey a sense of organization. All these elements come together to give the viewer a better understanding of the subject's character.
