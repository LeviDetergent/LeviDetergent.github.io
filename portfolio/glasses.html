Guitar
2021

Digital Media         2D Design and Color Concept         Illustration       About Me       Statement

My artwork centers on the subject's clothing and how it reflects their personality. I aimed to create a feminine-masculine style by giving the subject short hair, an oversized shirt, jeans, and sneakers. I used cool colors like blue, green, brown, and black to dye the clothing and create a tomboyish look. To make the piece more visually striking, I played around with color mixing techniques to add a glossy effect to the objects.
