RAIDEN

Adobe After Effect
Adobe Premiere Pro
Blender

2024

For this project, I wanted to practice animation in Blender. I used character models of Raiden (from Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance) and Johnny Silverhand (from Cyberpunk 2077), rigged them, and animated them. Since this was the first project I rendered in 4K, I wanted to test how long it would take my PC to complete the rendering. I designed the camera movement to feel like a benchmark tool, as I was testing the limits of my computer.

A benchmark tool is typically used to evaluate the performance of computer hardware by running intensive tasks, like gaming scenes or 3D rendering, while measuring speed, stability, and temperatures. By rendering this animation in 4K with detailed movement and effects, I treated the project as a personal benchmark to see how well my system could handle demanding visuals, making the render both a creative and technical challenge.
