Dancing
2021

This animation was made in Photoshop. I was inspired by a TikTok dance that I came across and decided to create an animation based on it. However, I wanted to add my own creative twist to it by incorporating transformation. So, I decided to make the dancer transform into a cupcake and then into a teddy bear. This experiment with animation transformation was exciting and challenging at the same time. I had to figure out the best way to transition smoothly from one form to the other.
